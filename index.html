Luigi Longo

Economist · European Commission — Joint Research Centre

I am an applied econometrician specialized in macroeconometrics and time series analysis. I hold a PhD in Economics from IMT Lucca and I currently work for the Joint Research Centre at the European Commission in the Nowcasting Team. I previously worked at the Bank of England and Boston University.
